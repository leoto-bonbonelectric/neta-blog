üõ° GFCI & AFCI Requirements

GFCI Required Locations ‚Äî NEC 210.8

  • Bathrooms (all receptacles)
  • Garages & accessory structures
  • Outdoors (all exterior receptacles)
  • Crawl spaces & unfinished basements
  • Kitchens (countertop receptacles within 6 ft of sink)
  • Sinks ‚Äî within 6 ft of any sink
  • Boathouses & boat hoists
  • Swimming pools, spas, hot tubs
  • Rooftops
  • Dishwashers (2020+ NEC)
  • Laundry areas & utility sinks
  • Receptacles in areas with standing water risk

AFCI Required Locations ‚Äî NEC 210.12

  • Bedrooms (all branch circuits)
  • Living rooms, parlors, libraries
  • Dens, sun rooms, recreation rooms
  • Kitchens & dining rooms (2020+ NEC)
  • Hallways & closets
  • Dormitory units
  • Guest rooms in hotels
  • All 120V, 15 & 20A branch circuits in dwellings (2020 NEC)

Note: AFCI + GFCI dual-function breakers available where both required.

  • Conduit fill: 40% max (3+ wires)
  • Voltage drop: 3% branch, 5% total
  • Continuous load = 80% of OCPD
  • Next size up rule: NEC 240.4(B)
  • Neutrals don't count as CCC if ‚â§50% 3rd harmonic
  • Receptacle spacing: every 12 ft / 6 ft from door

  • Smoke detectors: NEC 210.12 / 760
  • Tamper-resistant: all 15/20A 125V receptacles in dwellings
